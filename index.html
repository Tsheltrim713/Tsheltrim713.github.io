About Our Space

A Fusion of Korean & Japanese Cafe Culture

Welcome to Yoru공간 (Yoru Gonggan), where the tranquility of night meets the comfort of a cozy study space. Our cafe in Phuentsholing, Bhutan is inspired by the serene atmosphere of Korean and Japanese study cafes, blending the best of both cultures.

We've created a space where you can focus on your work or studies, enjoy delicious fusion drinks and meals, or simply relax with a good book. Our carefully curated menu combines traditional flavors with modern twists, offering something for every taste.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to study, a comfortable spot to work, or just want to unwind with friends, Yoru공간 is your perfect nighttime retreat.
